Welcome to PhotoForge

A local-first photo organizer. Everything runs on your PC — no cloud, no telemetry.

  1. Scan — pick a folder (e.g. your Pictures folder). PhotoForge indexes every photo's metadata and fingerprints. Re-scanning is fast: unchanged files are skipped.
  2. Duplicates — review exact and near-duplicate groups side by side and move the extras to a _Duplicates folder. Nothing is ever deleted, and every move can be undone.
  3. Review — let the classifier separate real photos from screenshots and app assets, then confirm or fix its guesses with the keyboard (Y / N / arrows).

System folders (Windows, Program Files, the Recycle Bin…) are skipped automatically. You can add your own skip folders on the Library screen.
